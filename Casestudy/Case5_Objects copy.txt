### **Case Study: Online Shopping Cart System Summary**

**Objective:** Develop an online shopping cart system for e-commerce that manages 
products, customer accounts, and shopping functionality.

**Key Components:**
1. **Product Object:** Attributes include ID, Name, Price, Stock Quantity; 
      methods for stock updates and discounts.
2. **Customer Object:** Attributes include ID, Name, Email, Password; 
      methods for account creation, login, and order history.
3. **Shopping Cart Object:** Manages a product list and total price;
      methods for adding, removing, and updating products.

**Challenges:** 
- Prevent overselling by managing stock.
- Validate stock limits during cart operations.
- Provide user-friendly feedback.

**Application:** Essential for e-commerce platforms, enhancing customer
 experience through efficient management of inventory and user accounts.
